## Hello, and welcome

My Name is Corey Vantilborg and I have been working as an ERP professional since 2010, which does not seem so long ago.  Fun fact,  the official kick-off of my first ERP project coincides roughly with the birth of my oldest son.   

This is my attempt to get back engaged with Dynamics community and eco-system.   I will be using it document my own learning about how to adopt and utilize the latest Business Application technology from Microsoft.  Hopefully it will provide value to the community. 



---

## What I'll be writing about

### Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations
Deep dives into F&O configuration, development patterns, and integration. Expect posts on data management, ALM practices, and the kind of hard-won specifics that don't always make it into the official docs.

### Microsoft Fabric & Data Architecture
I'm actively building out a medallion architecture for D365 data ingestion — bronze through gold. Topics will include Synapse Link patterns, incremental CSV ingestion, Delta Change Data Feed, and CDC from on-premises SQL Server.

### Azure Integration & AI Tooling
Connecting D365 and Fabric to the broader Azure ecosystem — API Management, Azure AI Foundry, and agentic patterns using Copilot Studio and MCP.

---

## Who this is for

If you're a D365 functional consultant, a data engineer working with Microsoft Fabric, or an architect trying to make sense of the MS Bizz Apps ecosystem— this blog is aimed at you. I'll try to keep posts practical and specific rather than high-level overviews.
